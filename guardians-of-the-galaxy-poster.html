Guardians of the Galaxy Poster

This is a movie poster I did for the film “Guardians of the Galaxy”. The goal was to create a poster using as many Photoshop effects as possible without making it look over the top. I chose to use the main character's mask as the focal point because he's wearing it the first time you see him (as an adult) in the movie.
